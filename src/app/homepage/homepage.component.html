Come join in the fun with hundreds of tech enthusiasts and business professionals in our annual most epic dev and biz conference up north. DevFest is a large community organised annual event with sessions & code labs for developers, students, startups and anyone interested to learn the latest trends and technical knowledge of Google platform, product APIs & open source technologies.

BizFest is the annual conference that aims to bring together professionals, business owners, entrepreneurs, startups, programmers and students to learn and share insights on innovations for the future.
